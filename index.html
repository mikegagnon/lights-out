• The goal is to turn all the lights off
  • A pink square is a light that is ON
  • A gray square is a light that is OFF
  • When you click a light, it toggles the following lights in a cross pattern:
    • The light you clicked
    • The light above the light you clicked
    • The light below the light you clicked
    • The light to the left of the light you clicked
    • The light to the right of the light you clicked
  • If there is no light below the light you clicked, then it wraps around (torus style) and toggles the light in the first row.
  • And so on for each of the four directions
